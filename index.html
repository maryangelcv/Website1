From Our Hands To Yours

From the selection of a unique stone to a personalized approach in our salons, finding
the perfect diamond jewel at Melora Diamonds passes the innate brilliance and allure of a

FOR YOUR DREAM RING...


We recover diamonds of the highest
quality

The Melora mine in Lesotho produces the highest quality gem diamonds,
consistently achieving the highest price per carat of any kimberlite mine in the
world. It is also famous for its large top colour white diamonds and has produced
three of the 20 largest white diamonds ever recorded since Melora Diamonds took
the mine over in 2006.

Engagement and Bridal Rings

We believe that an exquisite diamond ring is the most brilliant way to celebrate life's special
moments. Our extraordinary diamond rings begin with the natural beauty of a unique stone and
end with a jewel that will sparkle for a lifetime.
Guided by an exquisite center stone, the House's master artisans meticulously handset the
diamond into a minimal platinum setting to maximize its innate brilliance and sparkle. This
diamond-led approach is a signature of Melora craftsmanship, and what makes our
collection of Classic Melora engagement rings timeless treasures.
